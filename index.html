More About Me

I'm Zachary! I'm currently a second-year undergraduate in Computer Science at Brown University.

As a way of blending my interests in education and technology, I've served as a teaching assistant within the Computer Science department at Brown every semester.

In addition to funding from Brown University, my undergraduate studies have been generously supported by the CIT Group Scholarship. Additionally, my secondary school experience was completely free, having been funded by numerous generations of Regis High School alumni. (Thanks to everyone for helping me get to this point!)

Recently, I've been interested in musical theater, lighting design, inter-grader consistency on homework assignments, voting security (both in real-life and online), speech and debate, multiplayer game design, and Dance Dance Revolution.
